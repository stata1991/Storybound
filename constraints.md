# Storybound — Constraints

> Hard limits that cannot be changed without a major product rethink. Always apply these.

---

## Business Constraints

**Solo founder.** No team yet. Every decision must be executable by one person with contractors.

**Pre-revenue.** No external funding. Decisions must minimize burn until 50 paying families confirmed.

**2-month validation window.** If 50 paying founding families not confirmed by end of Phase 1, reassess before building further.

**Annual billing only.** No monthly billing option. Quarterly delivery ≠ quarterly billing. Parents pay upfront, receive 4 books across the year. This protects cash flow and reduces churn surface.

---

## Product Constraints

**Quarterly delivery cadence is fixed.** 4 books/year. Not monthly, not bi-monthly. Quarterly maps to natural seasonal memory clusters.

**Q4 is always the birthday book.** The birthday chapter is the emotional anchor of the year. It must arrive 1–2 weeks before the child's birthday. This is non-negotiable.

**Books must be print-quality.** Minimum 32 pages, hardcover preferred, full-color illustration. No compromising on physical quality — this is a premium keepsake product.

**Child must visually appear in illustrations.** Generic clip art or stock characters are not acceptable. The face generation must be consistent across all 4 books in a year.

**Story continuity is required.** Each quarterly book must reference the previous books in that year. Episode 4 must reference Episodes 1, 2, and 3. Year 2 must reference Year 1's climax.

---

## Privacy Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

**Session-based face processing only.** Child photos are processed to extract face embeddings, then deleted within 2 hours of face reference generation. Hard ceiling: 24 hours for failure cases only. No permanent storage of biometric data.

**No photo storage after processing.** Parents' uploaded photos are deleted after face model generation. Only the generated face reference (non-photographic) is retained.

**No face data or photos sent to any third party — ever.** The illustration pipeline runs exclusively on founder-controlled Modal.com infrastructure. Photos are never sent to OpenAI, Replicate, Midjourney, print partners, or any other third-party service.

**Children's data.** Full COPPA compliance required. No marketing to children. No behavioral tracking of minors. Parental consent required for all data collection.

**Photo usage:** Photos submitted are used only for that child's book generation. Never used for training data, marketing, or any other purpose without explicit opt-in.

---

## Content Constraints

**Age-appropriate content only.** All stories must be appropriate for ages 3–10. Violence, fear triggers, and mature themes are prohibited.

**Parent-specified avoidances are hard limits.** If a parent says "no spiders," there are zero spiders in any book for that child — ever. These are stored permanently and never overridden.

**No real-world locations or identifiable details.** Stories are set in fictional worlds. No real schools, neighborhoods, or identifiable family details in narrative.

**Cultural sensitivity.** No stereotypes, no culture-specific religious content without explicit opt-in, no assumptions about family structure (some children have two moms, single parents, grandparents as guardians).

---

## Operational Constraints

**Memory drop window: 3 weeks.** Parents have 3 weeks to submit photos and answers. After close, we proceed with what we have (or gracefully handle missing submissions).

**Late submissions policy:** After the memory drop window closes, we proceed with the previous quarter's reference data plus a "time passing" narrative note. Parent is informed, not penalized.

**Delivery timing:** Q1–Q3 must arrive within the seasonal window. Q4 must arrive 7–14 days before birthday — this is a hard deadline, not a guideline.

---

## Pricing Constraints

**Minimum viable price: $89/year.** Below this, unit economics don't work at target COGS. Do not discount below $89 even for validation.

**Founding member price: $89/year** (not lower). Full price: $109/year.

**Never compete on price.** Storybound is a premium product. Discounting trains customers to wait for sales. Compete on quality and continuity.

**Digital-only ($39/year) exists but is not marketed.** Available if a customer specifically requests it. Not featured on pricing page.
